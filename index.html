About the Lab

Computational chemistry for functional molecules and materials.

The T&CMC Research Group works at the interface of theoretical chemistry, molecular simulation, and computational materials design. The group integrates density functional theory, wave function based methods, atomistic simulations, high-throughput computation, and emerging machine learning strategies.

Our research focuses on molecular and materials systems where structure, dynamics, electronic properties, and chemical reactivity determine performance. We are especially interested in sustainable catalysis, gas separation, light matter interaction, photophysics, low dimensional materials, nanoclusters, surfaces, and data driven discovery.
