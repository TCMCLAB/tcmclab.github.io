# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-users" %} Team

The TCMC Research Group brings together researchers and students working in theoretical and computational materials chemistry. The group focuses on quantum chemical calculations, atomistic simulations, molecular modelling, and data driven approaches for understanding molecules and functional materials.

## Join and Collaborate

The group welcomes motivated students and collaborators interested in computational chemistry, molecular modelling, electronic structure theory, materials simulation, catalysis, photophysics, and data driven materials discovery.

For academic collaboration or student research enquiries, please use the contact page or reach out to the group through the official departmental channels.
