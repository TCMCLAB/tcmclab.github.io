# TCMC Research Group

Theoretical and Computational Materials Chemistry

The TCMC Research Group focuses on computational materials chemistry, combining first principles calculations, molecular simulations, and data driven modelling to understand structure, energetics, transport, and reactivity in functional materials.

{% include section.html %}

## Research Focus

{% capture text %}

Our group develops and applies atomistic simulation approaches to study materials for energy, environment, catalysis, adsorption, separation, and molecular recognition. We use methods such as density functional theory, molecular dynamics, Monte Carlo simulations, and cheminformatics based modelling to connect molecular level interactions with macroscopic material performance.
