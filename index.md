# Advancing Materials Discovery Through Computation

The **TCMC Research Group** focuses on theoretical and computational materials chemistry, using atomistic simulation and molecular modelling to understand the structure, energetics, transport, and reactivity of functional materials.

Our research connects molecular scale interactions with material level behaviour, supporting the rational design of systems for adsorption, separation, catalysis, energy, environment, and molecular recognition.

## Methods and Expertise

Our work integrates multiple computational approaches to study materials and molecular systems at different length and time scales.

- Density functional theory calculations
- Molecular dynamics simulations
- Monte Carlo and adsorption simulations
- Force field based molecular modelling
- Structure property analysis
- Transport and diffusion studies
- Gas adsorption and separation studies
- Computational catalysis and interfacial modelling
